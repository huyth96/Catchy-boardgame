Our Story

"Why do many learners remember words but forget how to use them?"
— Survey prompt that sparked Catchy
77%

Lacked a real practice environment

74%

Couldn't retain new words

66%

Didn't use idioms naturally

We interviewed 100+ students and discovered the same roadblocks. Catchy transforms study into play - every challenge, dare, and combo helps you speak first, remember later. When friends laugh together, vocabulary sticks.

What's Inside the Box?

Vocabulary Cards (A1-C2 levels) - meanings, examples, pronunciation.

Idioms Cards - fun, useful, easy to use in real talk.

Function Cards - attack, defend, combo to steer the flow.

Dare Cards - act, sing, react in English!

Every box links to our website for rules, examples, and gameplay videos.
